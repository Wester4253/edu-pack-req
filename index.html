Eligibility Requirements

📚 Student Status

Be enrolled in a degree or diploma granting course of study at an accredited educational institution.

🎓 Academic Email

Have a school-issued email address or upload documents that prove your current student status.

🆔 Verification

Be at least 13 years old and provide valid academic documentation for verification.

🌍 Global Access

Available to students worldwide at qualifying educational institutions.
